The Setup

Here's what we know for sure.

🏡
Home Base
Condesa, CDMX — one of the most walkable, beautiful, and food-obsessed neighborhoods in the city. Art Deco architecture, Parque México steps away, cocktail bars on every block.
📅
Our Days
Saturday — rolling arrivals, low-key night out
Sunday + Monday — two adventure days
Tuesday — museums open! + Taco Tour to close it all out
🌮
Tuesday Night: Non-Negotiable
We end every option the same way — a self-guided taco tour through the streets. No reservations. No plans. Just tacos.
⚠️
One Museum Note
Both the Anthropology Museum and Chapultepec Castle are closed Mondays. All our itineraries work around this — museum days always land on Sunday or Tuesday.
The Menu

Activities we're choosing from

🎈

Hot Air Balloon + Pyramids

Sunrise over Teotihuacán — one of the most jaw-dropping things you can do in all of Mexico. Breakfast buffet + optional pyramid walk included.

5am pickup · Half day · ~$150–200/pp
🏛️

Anthropology Museum

The finest pre-Columbian collection in the world. The Aztec Sun Stone. The Maya room. World-class and walkable from Condesa via Chapultepec Park.

Tue–Sun only · 3–4 hrs · ~$5/pp
🏰

Chapultepec Castle

The only royal castle in North America, perched on a hill with panoramic city views. Houses the National Museum of History — a steep but rewarding climb.

Tue–Sun only · 2–3 hrs · ~$5/pp
🚌

Turibus Hop-On Hop-Off

Four routes covering all of the city's major neighborhoods. Audio guides in 9 languages, stops near all major sights. Perfect intro to CDMX.

Daily 9am–7pm · Full day · ~$20/pp
🛶

Xochimilco Canal Tour

Colorful trajinera boats drifting through ancient Aztec canals — with floating vendors, mariachis, and snacks rowing up alongside you. Pure joy.

Daily 8am–5pm · Half–full day
🍽️

High-End Group Dinner

One special evening at a restaurant with a private dining room for our group. Think top-tier Mexican cuisine — contemporary or traditional. Worth the splurge.

Sun or Mon evening · ~$150–200/pp

Good to Know

A few things before you vote.

🎈 Hot Air Balloon
5am pickup from near the Airbnb — plan accordingly the night before
Top-rated company: Volare (FAA + AFAC certified, thousands of 5-star reviews)
Includes champagne toast, buffet breakfast + optional 2hr pyramid time
Eats most of the morning — plan a slow afternoon after
🏛️ Museums
Both museums closed Mondays — this is locked in and planned around
Anthropology Museum: open Tue–Sun, 9am–5pm — budget 3–4 hrs minimum
Chapultepec Castle: open Tue–Sun, 9am–5pm — steep uphill walk, totally worth it
Both are in Chapultepec Park — can do both in one day comfortably
🛶 Xochimilco
A UNESCO World Heritage canal system — ancient Aztec chinampas
Trajinera boats priced per boat, not per person — good for groups!
Bring cash for floating vendors, mariachis, and canal-side snacks
Plan half to full day — pairs beautifully with Coyoacán neighborhood after
🍽️ Dinner & 🌮 Taco Tour
High-end dinner: private dining room likely needed for our group size — restaurant TBD pending headcount
Budget range: ~$150–200/pp (could be a bit less depending on choice)
Tuesday Taco Tour is completely self-guided — no booking, no bus, just us and CDMX
Condesa / Roma Norte are super safe neighborhoods for walking at night
